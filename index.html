Bluefold

A PDF reader for people who live in textbooks.

Open your whole library at once. Navigate like a browser. Find anything again — even in scanned pages.

Download for Mac

macOS 15 or later · Apple silicon & Intel

Open everything

Only the tab you're reading holds a live document — background tabs keep their place and reload lazily. 10 GB of textbooks open at once is the point, not a stress test.

Navigate like a browser

Tabs, windows, and per-tab back/forward history. ⌘-click an internal link to open it in a new tab; ⌘⇧T reopens what you just closed.

Pick up where you left off

Quit and relaunch to exactly the windows, tabs, and positions you had. New windows open in the macOS Space you're actually in.

A library with real tags

Hierarchical tags and collections across textbooks, papers, and homework. Attach an existing Calibre library as a read-only source — Bluefold never touches it.

Search books you've never opened

A background full-text index covers your whole library, with OCR for scanned PDFs. Live results as you type — no Enter key required.

Easy on the eyes

Light, dark, and sepia themes applied to the PDF page itself — smart-inverted or warm-paper rendering, not just the window chrome.

Built keyboard-first

  • Command palette: ⌘O open anything, ⌘P jump within the book, ⌘⇧P run commands
  • Split view — two books or two places in one book, side by side (⌘\)
  • Margin anchors — copyable deep links to any theorem, section, or definition
  • bluefold:// links that open the right book to the right page from your notes
  • Watched folders — exports (e.g. from a reMarkable) appear and refresh automatically
  • Customizable keybindings via a simple JSON file
